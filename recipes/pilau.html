Pilau Recipe

Ingredients:

Serves 2 people

  • 150 g frozen mixed vegetables(thawed)
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ vegetable stock cube
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 300 ml boiling water
  • salt and black pepper(to season)

Steps

  1. Place the rice, turmeric, garam masala, butter, thawed vegetables and a pinch of salt and pepper in a microwave-proof bowl.
  2. Crumble the half stock cube over, then pour 300ml boiling water into the bowl. Stir to mix everything.
  3. Cover the bowl with clingfilm, poking a hole with a fork or knife.
  4. Microwave the rice on high for 7 minutes, then on medium for 5 minutes.
